## How to Provide Feedback

If you've been asked to provide feedback for this self-assessment, please
consider:

- **Technical leadership** – guiding direction, making decisions
- **Team enablement** – helping others, removing blockers
- **Stakeholder trust** – being the go-to person
- **Communication** – clarity, proactivity, transparency
- **Ownership** – accountability beyond assigned tasks
- **Impact** – measurable outcomes or improvements
